                              ADJOURNMENT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to section 5(a)(1)(B) of House 
Resolution 8, and pursuant to House Resolution 155, the House stands 
adjourned until 10 a.m. tomorrow for morning-hour debate and noon for 
legislation business, as a further mark of respect to the memory of the 
late Honorable Ronald J. Wright.
  Thereupon (at 6 o'clock and 25 minutes p.m.), under its previous 
order, the House adjourned until tomorrow, Thursday, February 25, 2021, 
at 10 a.m., as a further mark of respect to the memory of the late 
Honorable Ronald J. Wright.
