                             Cloture Motion

  We, the undersigned Senators, in accordance with the provisions of 
rule XXII of the Standing Rules of the Senate, do hereby move to bring 
to a close debate on Executive Calendar No. 8, Gina Marie Raimondo, of 
Rhode Island, to be Secretary of Commerce.
         Charles E. Schumer, Sherrod Brown, Sheldon Whitehouse, 
           Benjamin L. Cardin, Robert Menendez, Patrick J. Leahy, 
           Alex Padilla, Jacky Rosen, Richard J. Durbin, Tammy 
           Baldwin, Jack Reed, Chris Van Hollen, Richard 
           Blumenthal, Tim Kaine, Martin Heinrich, Christopher 
           Murphy, Maria Cantwell.

  Mr. SCHUMER. I ask unanimous consent that the mandatory quorum call 
for the cloture motion be waived.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
