                             Cloture Motion

  Mr. SCHUMER. Madam President, I send a cloture motion to the desk.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The cloture motion having been presented under 
rule XXII, the Chair directs the clerk to read the motion.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows

                             Cloture Motion

       We, the undersigned Senators, in accordance with the 
     provisions of rule XXII of the Standing Rules of the Senate, 
     do hereby move to bring to a close debate on the nomination 
     of Executive Calendar No. 13, Cecilia Elena Rouse, of New 
     Jersey, to be Chairman of the Council of Economic Advisers.
         Charles E. Schumer, Sherrod Brown, Tina Smith, Tammy 
           Baldwin, Thomas R. Carper, Sheldon Whitehouse, Patrick 
           J. Leahy, Brian Schatz, Christopher A. Coons, Jack 
           Reed, Michael F. Bennet, Debbie Stabenow, Chris Van 
           Hollen, Ron Wyden, Martin Heinrich, Bernard Sanders, 
           Edward J. Markey, Cory A. Booker.

  Mr. SCHUMER. I ask unanimous consent that the mandatory quorum call 
for the cloture motion be waived.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
  The Senator from Wyoming.
  Mr. BARRASSO. Madam President, I ask unanimous consent that I be 
allowed to conclude my remarks before recessing.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
