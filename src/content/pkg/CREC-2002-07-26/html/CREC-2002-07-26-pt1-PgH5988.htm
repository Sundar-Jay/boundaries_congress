                              ADJOURNMENT

  Mr. ARMEY. Mr. Speaker, pursuant to Senate Concurrent Resolution 132, 
107th Congress, I move that the House do now adjourn.
  The motion was agreed to.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to Senate Concurrent Resolution 
132, 107th Congress, the House stands adjourned until 2 p.m., 
Wednesday, September 4, 2002.
  Thereupon (at 3 o'clock and 40 minutes a.m.), Saturday, July 27, 
2002, legislative day of Friday, July 26, 2002, pursuant to Senate 
Concurrent Resolution 132, the House adjourned until Wednesday, 
September 4, 2002, at 2 p.m.
