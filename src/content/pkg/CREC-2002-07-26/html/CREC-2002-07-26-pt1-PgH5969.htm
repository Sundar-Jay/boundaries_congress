REMOVAL OF CONFEREE AND APPOINTMENT OF CONFEREE ON H.R. 3210, TERRORISM 
                          RISK PROTECTION ACT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Simpson). Without objection and pursuant 
to clause 11, rule I, the Chair removes the gentleman from North 
Carolina (Mr. Coble) as a conferee on H.R. 3210, Terrorism Risk 
Protection Act, and appoints the gentleman from Virginia (Mr. 
Goodlatte) to fill the vacancy.
  There was no objection.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Clerk will notify the Senate of the 
change in conferees.
