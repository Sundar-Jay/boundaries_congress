             ELECTION OF MEMBER TO COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE

  Mr. ARMEY. Mr. Speaker, I offer a resolution (H. Res. 510) and I ask 
unanimous consent for its immediate consideration in the House.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. The Clerk will report the resolution.
  The Clerk read as follows:

                              H. Res. 510

       Resolved, That the following Member be and is hereby 
     elected to the following standing committee of the House of 
     Representatives:

       Agriculture: Mr. Gekas of Pennsylvania.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Is there objection to the request of the 
gentleman from Texas?
  There was no objection.
  The resolution was agreed to.
  A motion to reconsider was laid on the table.
