                           EXECUTIVE CALENDAR

  Mr. DASCHLE. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the Senate 
proceed to executive session to consider the following nominations: 
Calendar Nos. 852, 868, 869, 870, 871, 872, 873, 874, 875, 877, 878, 
879, 880, 881, 882, and 883; that the nominations be confirmed, the 
motions to reconsider be laid upon the table; that any statements 
relating thereto be printed in the Record; that the President be 
immediately notified of the Senate's action; and that the Senate then 
return to legislative session, with the preceding all occurring without 
any intervening action or debate.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
