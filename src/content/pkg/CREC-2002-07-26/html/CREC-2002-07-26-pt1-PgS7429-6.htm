                        ENROLLED BILL PRESENTED

  The Secretary of the Senate reported that on today, July 26, 2002, 
she had presented to the President of the United States the following 
enrolled bill:

       S.J. Res. 13. A joint resolution conferring honorary 
     citizenship of the United States posthumously on Marie Joseph 
     Paul Yves Roche Gilbert du Motier, the Marquis de Lafayette.
