  APPOINTMENT OF MEMBER TO UNITED STATES GROUP OF THE NORTH ATLANTIC 
                                ASSEMBLY

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, and pursuant to 22 U.S.C. 
1928a and clause 10 of rule I, the Chair announces the Speaker's 
appointment of the following Member of the House to the United States 
Group of the North Atlantic Assembly to fill the existing vacancy 
thereon:
  Mr. Tanner of Tennessee.
  There was no objection.
