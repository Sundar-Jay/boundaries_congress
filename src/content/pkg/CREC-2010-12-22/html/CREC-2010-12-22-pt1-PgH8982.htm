 APPOINTMENT--NATIONAL ADVISORY COMMITTEE ON INSTITUTIONAL QUALITY AND 
                               INTEGRITY

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to section 106 of the Higher 
Education Opportunity Act (P.L. 110-315) and the order of the House of 
January 6, 2009, the Chair announces the Speaker's appointment of the 
following Member on the part of the House to the National Advisory 
Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity for a term of 6 years:
  Upon the recommendation of the majority leader:
  Dr. George T. French, Fairfield, Alabama.
