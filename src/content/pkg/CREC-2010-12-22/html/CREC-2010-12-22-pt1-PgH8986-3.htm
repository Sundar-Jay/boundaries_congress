                          SINE DIE ADJOURNMENT

  Mr. McDERMOTT. Madam Speaker, I move that the House do now adjourn.
  The motion was agreed to.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore. In accordance with House Concurrent 
Resolution 336, 111th Congress, the Chair declares the Second Session 
of the 111th Congress adjourned sine die.
  Accordingly (at 6 p.m.), the House adjourned.
