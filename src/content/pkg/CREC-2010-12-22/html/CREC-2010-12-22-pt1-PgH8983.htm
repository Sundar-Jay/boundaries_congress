    APPOINTMENT--ADVISORY COMMITTEE ON STUDENT FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Pursuant to section 491 of the Higher 
Education Act (20 U.S.C. 1098(c)), as amended, and the order of the 
House of January 6, 2009, the Chair announces the Speaker's appointment 
of the following member on the part of the House to the Advisory 
Committee on Student Financial Assistance for a term of 4 years:
  Upon the recommendation of the majority leader:
  Ms. Deborah Stanley, Bowie, Maryland.
