 SENATE RESOLUTION 290--APPOINTING A COMMITTEE TO NOTIFY THE PRESIDENT 
           CONCERNING THE PROPOSED ADJOURNMENT OF THE SESSION

  Mr. MITCHELL (for himself and Mr. Dole) submitted the following 
resolution; which was considered and agreed to:

                              S. Res. 290

       Resolved, That a committee of two Senators be appointed by 
     the Presiding Office to join a similar committee of the House 
     of Representatives to notify the President of the United 
     States that the two Houses have completed their business of 
     the session and are ready to adjourn unless he has some 
     further communication to make to them.
