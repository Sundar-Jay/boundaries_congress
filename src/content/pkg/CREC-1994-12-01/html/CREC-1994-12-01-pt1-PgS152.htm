      COMMENDING THE EXEMPLARY LEADERSHIP OF THE REPUBLICAN LEADER

  Mr. MITCHELL. Mr. President, acting in the belief that one good 
resolution deserves another, I send a resolution to the desk and ask 
for its immediate consideration.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The clerk will report.
  The legislative clerk read as follows:

       A resolution (S. Res. 294) to commend the exemplary 
     leadership of the Republican Leader.

  There being no objection, the Senate proceeded to consider the 
resolution.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The question is on agreeing to the resolution.
  The resolution (S. Res. 294) was agreed to as follows:

                              S. Res. 294

       Resolved, That the thanks of the Senate are hereby tendered 
     to the distinguished Republican leader, the Senator from 
     Kansas, the Honorable Robert Dole, for his exemplary 
     leadership and the cooperative and dedicated manner in which 
     he has performed his leadership responsibilities in the 
     conduct of Senate business during the second session of the 
     103d Congress.
