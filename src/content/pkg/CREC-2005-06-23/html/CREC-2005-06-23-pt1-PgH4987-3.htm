                          PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE

  The SPEAKER. Will the gentlewoman from California (Ms. Linda T. 
Sanchez) come forward and lead the House in the Pledge of Allegiance.
  Ms. LINDA T. SANCHEZ of California led the Pledge of Allegiance as 
follows:

       I pledge allegiance to the Flag of the United States of 
     America, and to the Republic for which it stands, one nation 
     under God, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all.
