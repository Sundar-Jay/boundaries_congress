 REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 8, DEATH TAX 
                        ELIMINATION ACT OF 2000

  Mr. REYNOLDS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged 
report (Rept. No. 106-658) on the resolution (H. Res. 519) providing 
for consideration of the bill (H.R. 8) to amend the Internal Revenue 
Code of 1986 to phase out the estate and gift taxes over a 10-year 
period, which was referred to the House Calendar and ordered to be 
printed.
