VISIT TO THE SENATE BY THEIR MAJESTIES KING ABDULLAH II AND QUEEN RANIA 
             AL-ABDULLAH OF THE HASHEMITE KINGDOM OF JORDAN

  Mr. HELMS. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent the Senate stand in 
recess for 7 minutes so the Senators may pay their respects to the 
Honorable King of Jordan and his lovely lady.
  There being no objection, the Senate, at 4:56 p.m. recessed until 
5:04 p.m.; whereupon the Senate reassembled when called to order by the 
Presiding Officer (Mr. Voinovich).
