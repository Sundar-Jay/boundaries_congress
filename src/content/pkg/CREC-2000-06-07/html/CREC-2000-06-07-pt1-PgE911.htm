                         TRIBUTE TO JOSEY KUNZ

                                 ______
                                 

                           HON. JOHN SHIMKUS

                              of illinois

                    in the house of representatives

                        Wednesday, June 7, 2000

  Mr. SHIMKUS. Mr. Speaker, I rise before you today to recognize Josey 
Kunz of Bluffs, IL. Josey, a fourth grader from Bluffs Elementary 
School was one of only four State Organ/Tissue Donor Poster Contest 
winners.
  I would like to take this opportunity to congratulate Josey for his 
talent and accomplishment. He is an exceptional young man who has made 
me and the people of my district proud.
