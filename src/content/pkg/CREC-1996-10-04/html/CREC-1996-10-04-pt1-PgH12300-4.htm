                          SINE DIE ADJOURNMENT

  Mr. MYERS of Indiana. Mr. Speaker, the work of the 2d session of the 
104th Congress has been completed. Pursuant to House Concurrent 
Resolution 230, as amended, I move that the House do now adjourn.
  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Walker). In accordance with the 
provisions of House Concurrent Resolution 230, as amended, the Chair 
declares the 2d session of the 104th Congress adjourned sine die.
  Thereupon (at 2 o'clock and 52 minutes p.m.), pursuant to House 
Concurrent Resolution 230, as amended, the House adjourned.
