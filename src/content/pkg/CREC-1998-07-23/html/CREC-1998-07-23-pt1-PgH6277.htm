REPORT ON RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR CONSIDERATION OF H.R. 4250, PATIENT 
                         PROTECTION ACT OF 1998

  Mr. GOSS, from the Committee on Rules, submitted a privileged report 
(Rept. No. 105-643) on the resolution (H.Res. 509) providing for 
consideration of the bill (H.R. 4250) to provide new patient 
protections under group health plans, which was referred to the House 
CALENDAR and ordered to be printed.
