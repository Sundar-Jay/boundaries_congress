                           NOTICE OF HEARING


                      committee on indian affairs

  Mr. CAMPBELL. Mr. President, I would like to announce that the Senate 
Committee on Indian Affairs will meet during the session of the Senate 
on Wednesday, July 29, 1998, at 9:30 a.m. to conduct a Business Meeting 
to consider the following pending business of the Committee: S. 1905, A 
Bill to Compensate the Cheyenne River Sioux Tribe, and for Other 
Purposes; S. 391, To Provide for the Distribution of Certain Judgment 
Funds to the Mississippi Sioux Tribe of Indians, and for Other 
Purposes; and S. 1770, To Elevate the Position of the Director of the 
Indian Health Service to Assistant Secretary for Health and Human 
Services. The Business Meeting will be held in room 485 of the Russell 
Senate Office Building.
  Those wishing additional information should contact the Committee on 
Indian Affairs at 202/224-2251.
