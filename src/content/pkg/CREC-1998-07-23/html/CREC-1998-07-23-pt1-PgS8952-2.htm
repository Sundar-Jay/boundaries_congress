                                PROGRAM

  Mr. SHELBY. For the information of all Senators, tomorrow, at 9:15 
a.m., the Senate will vote on passage of the transportation 
appropriations bill. Following that vote, the Senate will begin 
consideration of the credit union legislation. The leader has announced 
that any votes ordered with respect to the credit union bill or any 
other legislative or executive items will be postponed, to occur on 
Monday, July 27, at a time to be determined by the two leaders.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
